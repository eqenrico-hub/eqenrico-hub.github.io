Click a shape button, then click-drag to draw (PowerPoint-style bbox). Hold Alt = from center · Shift = lock square/circle.
Move: drag body · Shift=lock axis · Alt=skip snap-guides
Resize: drag the 8 yellow handles · Shift+corner = lock aspect ratio
Guides: click ＋ Guide H / ＋ Guide V to add personal rulers (not exported in JSON)
Shortcuts: Cmd+Z undo · Cmd+Shift+Z redo · Cmd+C/V copy/paste · Cmd+D duplicate · Cmd+X cut · Delete · Arrows nudge · Shift+Arrows nudge 10px · Esc deselect
