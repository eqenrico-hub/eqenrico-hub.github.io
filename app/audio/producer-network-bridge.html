What It Does

A platform where music producers sign up through a beautiful 7-step onboarding flow and get matched with similar producers based on genre, favorite artists, production style, and career stage. Think "Tinder for producer collaborations" with a sophisticated multi-factor matching algorithm.

Problem It Solves

Finding collaborators in music production is hard. Producers often work in isolation without knowing other artists who share their sound, genre, or creative vision. This platform uses intelligent matching based on real SoundCloud network connections to connect producers who would actually vibe together.

Key Features

  • Beautiful 7-step animated onboarding with Framer Motion
  • SoundCloud profile validation and network import
  • Spotify artist search for favorite artists
  • Genre multi-select with custom genre support
  • DAW selection (Ableton, FL, Logic, Bitwig, etc.)
  • Production style sliders (Heavy/Melodic, Minimal/Layered)
  • Career stage matching (from beginner to pro)
  • Connection goals (Collabs, Feedback, Samples, Labels, Networking)
  • Match tiers: Perfect (90+), Great (75-89), Good (60-74)
  • Accept/decline match system with SoundCloud links

Matching Algorithm

MATCH SCORE = 100 points max 35% GRAPH SIMILARITY (max 35 pts) ├── Jaccard similarity on SoundCloud followings └── More shared connections = higher score 30% CONTENT SIMILARITY (max 30 pts) ├── Genre overlap: 5 pts each, max 15 ├── Artist overlap: 2 pts each, max 10 └── DAW match: 5 pts 20% COLLABORATIVE POTENTIAL (max 20 pts) ├── Style compatibility: 10 pts └── Shared goals: 2 pts each, max 10 15% CAREER ALIGNMENT (max 15 pts) ├── Same tier: 15 pts ├── Adjacent tier: 10 pts └── 2 tiers apart: 5 pts

Onboarding Flow

  1. Welcome & Email - Email capture with feature overview
  2. SoundCloud Link - Validate and import profile
  3. Genre Selection - Multi-select pills with custom option
  4. Favorite Artists - Spotify autocomplete (3-10 artists)
  5. Production Style - DAW + style sliders
  6. Career Stage - Auto-suggest based on followers
  7. Connection Goals - What you're looking for + frequency

Why I Built This

The music production world is surprisingly isolated despite the internet connecting everyone. Producers often work alone, not knowing other artists who share their exact vibe. I wanted to create a platform that uses the real connections on SoundCloud (who follows who) combined with musical preferences to make genuinely useful matches - not just random suggestions, but producers you'd actually want to collaborate with.
