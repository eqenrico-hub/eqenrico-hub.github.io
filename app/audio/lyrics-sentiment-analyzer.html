What It Does

Analyzes song lyrics to detect emotional content, themes, and sentiment patterns. Visualizes the emotional journey of songs and albums.

Problem It Solves

Understanding the emotional arc of music helps with playlist curation, music recommendation, and academic analysis of lyrical content.

Key Features

  • Multi-emotion detection (joy, sadness, anger, etc.)
  • Verse-by-verse sentiment breakdown
  • Album-wide emotional arc visualization
  • Theme and topic extraction
  • Artist comparison analysis

Tech Stack

Python NLTK Transformers Plotly

Why I Built This

As a musician, I'm fascinated by how lyrics convey emotion. This tool helps me understand and improve my own songwriting.
