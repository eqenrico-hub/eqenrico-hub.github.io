What It Does

A comprehensive analysis platform examining 114 Italian urban dashboards (UDs) to understand how cities present data to citizens and measure public sector transparency.

Problem It Solves

No systematic analysis exists of how Italian cities use dashboards for civic communication. This research fills the gap by cataloging and analyzing dashboard implementations.

Key Features

  • Database of 114 Italian urban dashboards
  • Classification by data types and visualization methods
  • Accessibility and usability scoring
  • Comparative analysis across municipalities
  • Academic research output generation

Tech Stack

Python Pandas Selenium Jupyter

Why I Built This

Part of my PhD research on public sector accountability. Understanding how cities communicate with citizens through data is crucial for improving democratic transparency.
