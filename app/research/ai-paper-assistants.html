What It Does

A suite of AI-powered tools to assist with academic paper writing, including literature review, citation management, and writing assistance tailored for scholarly work.

Problem It Solves

Academic writing has unique requirements (citations, formal tone, structured arguments) that general AI assistants don't handle well. This provides specialized support for researchers.

Key Features

  • Literature review assistance
  • Citation suggestion and formatting
  • Academic tone checking
  • Abstract and summary generation
  • Research gap identification

Tech Stack

Python LangChain OpenAI Semantic Scholar API

Why I Built This

As a PhD student, I wanted AI tools that understand the specific needs of academic writing rather than generic text generation.
