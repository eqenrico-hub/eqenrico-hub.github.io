What It Does

A comprehensive strategic intelligence system for FOQUS (Fondazione Quartieri Spagnoli), a private urban regeneration foundation in Naples. Provides data-driven insights for fundraising, benchmarking, stakeholder mapping, and strategic decision-making.

Problem It Solves

Urban regeneration foundations need intelligence on funding opportunities, comparable projects, and stakeholder networks. This platform consolidates research across multiple domains to support strategic planning.

Key Features

  • Power and influence mapping (foundations, funders, media, universities)
  • Funding and grants intelligence (EU, private foundations, corporate)
  • Strategic benchmarking against similar European projects
  • Territory and context analysis (social risk, needs, narratives)
  • Institutional memory and positioning
  • Decision synthesis dashboards

Tech Stack

Python Streamlit Web Scraping AI Analysis

Context

FOQUS is a private urban regeneration project active since 2014 in the Quartieri Spagnoli (Spanish Quarters) of Naples - one of Europe's most dense and fragile urban contexts. It integrates real estate recovery, business incubation, education, community welfare, and cultural industries with 136 jobs created and 1000+ direct beneficiaries.

Why I Built This

To support evidence-based strategic planning for social impact organizations working in complex urban environments.
