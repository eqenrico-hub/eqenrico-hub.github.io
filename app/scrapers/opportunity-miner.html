What It Does

Scrapes job postings, grants, and funding opportunities from multiple sources and uses AI to match them with your profile and interests.

Problem It Solves

Relevant opportunities are scattered across dozens of websites. This consolidates them and uses smart matching to surface the best fits for your profile.

Key Features

  • Multi-platform opportunity scraping
  • Profile-based matching algorithm
  • Deadline tracking and alerts
  • Application requirement extraction
  • Priority scoring

Tech Stack

Python Scrapy PostgreSQL FastAPI

Why I Built This

As a PhD student, I'm always looking for grants and opportunities. Manual searching is inefficient, so I automated it.
