What It Does

A unified intelligence platform that aggregates AI news, scans for AI influencers, generates AI podcasts with realistic voices and music, and monitors product launches across multiple sources. Deployed on Railway with a Next.js frontend and multiple microservices.

Problem It Solves

Keeping up with the AI ecosystem requires monitoring dozens of sources daily. This platform automates the entire pipeline: research, summarization, podcast creation, influencer discovery, and product intelligence — all from one dashboard.

Platform Modules

Module What It Does
PodcastAI Generates AI podcasts with Bark TTS voices, MusicGen intro/outro/jingles, 12 podcast vibes, topic memory across episodes
AI News Aggregator Smart research with Tavily + OpenAI, deduplication, quality scoring, multi-topic tracking
Influencer Scanner Discovers AI influencers and thought leaders across platforms
Product Intelligence Monitors Product Hunt, GitHub, Hacker News, Reddit for new launches and trends
Opportunity Miner YouTube scanner for product and business opportunities

PodcastAI Features

  • Bark TTS - Expressive AI voices with laughter, emotion tags, male/female selection
  • MusicGen - AI-generated intro, outro, section jingles, and background music per vibe
  • 12 Podcast Vibes - Morning Coffee, Hype Bros, News Anchor, Late Night, TED Talk, British Doc, and more
  • Dual Hosts - Two AI hosts with distinct voices and conversational banter
  • Episode Memory - Tracks what was covered to avoid repetition across episodes
  • Duration Control - Quick (2min), Standard (5min), Deep Dive (10min), Marathon (30min)
  • Background Generation - Close the page, podcast keeps generating and saves automatically
  • Background Music - Auto-ducking background music that lowers during speech

Tech Stack

Next.js 14 TypeScript Firebase (Auth + Firestore + Storage) Replicate API (Bark TTS + MusicGen) OpenAI GPT-4o Tavily Search API FFmpeg Railway Server-Sent Events (SSE)

Architecture

  • Frontend - Next.js React SPA with SSE streaming for real-time progress
  • Backend - Next.js API routes with long-running generation (no timeout limits on Railway)
  • Audio Pipeline - Research > Script > Critique > Revision > TTS > Music > FFmpeg Assembly > Upload
  • Caching - Music clips cached in Firebase Storage with signed URLs to avoid re-generation
  • Auth - Google Sign-In with Firebase Auth, tiered subscriptions (Free/Pro/Premium)
