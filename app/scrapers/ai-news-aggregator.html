What It Does

A comprehensive AI research assistant that transforms your objectives into actionable guides. Upload PDFs, get AI-generated expert personas who analyze the literature, and receive both academic and practical step-by-step guides. Now with Obsidian export and AI podcast generation.

Problem It Solves

Research is overwhelming. You have dozens of PDFs, scattered notes, and no clear path forward. This system creates virtual expert panels who synthesize literature, answer your questions, and produce actionable implementation guides.

Key Features

  • 10-Stage Research Pipeline - From objective to implementation guide
  • AI Expert Personas - Virtual experts for each topic with generated portraits
  • PDF Intelligence - Extract key findings from academic papers
  • Dynamic Extraction Schemas - Custom fields based on your objective
  • Two Guide Types - Literature-grounded (academic) + Simple step-by-step (practical)
  • Interactive Expert Chat - Ask follow-up questions to your panel
  • Obsidian Export - Full vault with backlinks, frontmatter, and graph-ready structure
  • AI Podcast Generation - Convert guides to audio with TTS (single narrator or two-host discussion)
  • Cost Tracking - Real-time budget monitoring in EUR

Output Formats

  • Word Documents - Literature guide + Simple guide (.docx)
  • Obsidian Vault - Topics, articles, experts, guides with wikilinks
  • Audio Podcasts - Single narrator or two-host discussion format
  • JSON Exports - All extracted data for programmatic use

Tech Stack

API Costs

  • OpenAI GPT-4 - ~$0.03/1K tokens (main reasoning)
  • OpenAI GPT-4 Mini - ~$0.0004/1K tokens (fast tasks)
  • OpenAI TTS HD - ~$0.03/1K characters (podcast audio)
  • OpenAI Image Gen - ~$0.02/image (expert portraits)
  • ElevenLabs - Optional, higher quality TTS (~$0.30/1K chars)

Built-in cost tracking with budget alerts. Typical research session: $2-5 EUR.

Why I Built This

PhD research means reading hundreds of papers across multiple topics. I needed a system that could synthesize literature into actionable steps, with experts I could consult. The podcast feature lets me "listen to my research" during commutes. Obsidian export creates a permanent knowledge base that grows with each project.
