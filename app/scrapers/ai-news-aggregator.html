What It Does

Automatically scrapes AI and technology news from multiple sources, summarizes articles using AI, and delivers curated daily digests.

Problem It Solves

Keeping up with AI news is overwhelming. This filters signal from noise and provides concise summaries of the most important developments.

Key Features

  • Multi-source news scraping
  • AI-powered summarization
  • Topic categorization
  • Daily digest emails
  • Importance scoring

Tech Stack

Python BeautifulSoup OpenAI Celery

Why I Built This

I wanted to stay informed about AI developments without spending hours reading. Automated curation lets me focus on what matters most.
