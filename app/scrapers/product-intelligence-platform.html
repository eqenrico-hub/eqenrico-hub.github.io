What It Does

Scrapes product information, pricing, and reviews from e-commerce platforms. Provides competitive intelligence and market analysis.

Problem It Solves

Understanding market positioning and competitive pricing requires constant monitoring. This automates the data collection and provides actionable insights.

Key Features

  • Multi-platform product scraping
  • Price tracking and alerts
  • Review sentiment analysis
  • Competitor monitoring
  • Market trend reports

Tech Stack

Python Selenium MongoDB Streamlit

Why I Built This

I wanted to understand how AI tools and products are priced and marketed. This platform provides the data for that analysis.
