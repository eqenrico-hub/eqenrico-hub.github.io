What It Does

A digital version of the Flip7 card game. Play against friends or AI opponents in this strategic card-flipping game.

Key Features

  • Local multiplayer support
  • AI opponents
  • Score tracking
  • Animated card flips
  • Mobile-friendly interface

Tech Stack

React TypeScript Vite

Why I Built This

My friends and I love playing Flip7 but we don't always have the physical cards. This lets us play anywhere.
