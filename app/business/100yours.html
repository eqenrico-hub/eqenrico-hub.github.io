What It Does

100yours.com is a platform that gives music producers their own branded website with a retro desktop OS interface. Each producer gets a fully customizable site with a beats store, merch shop, audio player, photo gallery, contact form, and more — all inside a draggable window manager that looks like Windows XP, Mac OS 9, Win98, or other classic operating systems.

Live: 100yours.com

Problem It Solves

Producers on BeatStars, Airbit, and Traktrain all look the same — template-locked, competing with other sellers on the same page, and paying monthly fees forever. 100yours gives producers a unique visual identity (retro OS themes), full ownership ($49 one-time, no subscriptions), and a page where only their beats appear.

Key Features

  • 7 OS themes — Windows XP, Win98, Mac OS 9, Leopard, Win7, System 1 (1984), BIOS Mode
  • Beats Store — up to 120 beats, audio auto-compressed to 128kbps, cover images auto-compressed
  • Merch Store — unlimited products with Stripe + PayPal checkout
  • Loops Store — subscription section + loop items with audio preview
  • Desktop MP3s — drag-and-drop MP3 icons on the desktop with player windows
  • Video Player — 7 themed media player skins (Winamp, WMP12, QuickTime, HyperCard, etc.)
  • Photo Dump — up to 50 photos, auto-compressed
  • Draw Window — visitors submit drawings, producer approves/deletes
  • Audio Tagline Watermark — voice tag plays over all audio at configurable intervals
  • Per-window styling — 130+ Google Fonts, 20 colors, 12 gradients, zoom control
  • Custom templates — save/load theme + icon + wallpaper snapshots
  • Free-form icon positioning — drag desktop icons anywhere
  • About window — 5 layouts: Classic, D&D Character Sheet, Terminal, Trading Card, Business Card
  • Visualizer — audio-reactive bars, wave, circular, mirror, particles
  • Undo/Redo — 20-action DB snapshot-based undo system
  • 500MB storage quota per site with usage tracking

Business Model

  • Price: $49 one-time (no monthly fees, ever)
  • Hosting: included, on Cloudflare Pages (free tier, unlimited bandwidth)
  • Revenue: producers keep 100% of their sales (Stripe/PayPal goes directly to them)
  • Competitive edge: custom domain included at $49 — BeatStars charges €209/year for this

Why I Built This

As a music producer myself, I was frustrated by how every BeatStars page looks identical. I wanted to give producers a website that actually reflects their visual identity — something fun, memorable, and entirely theirs. The retro OS concept came from the idea that a producer's website should feel like entering their world, not browsing a template catalog.
