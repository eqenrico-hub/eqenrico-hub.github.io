What It Does

A specialized route planning system for oversized and exceptional transport in Italy. It calculates optimal routes while considering infrastructure constraints like bridge weight limits, tunnel heights, and road width restrictions.

Problem It Solves

Companies transporting oversized cargo (wind turbines, industrial equipment, prefab buildings) need to plan routes that avoid infrastructure that can't handle their load. Currently this is done manually, which is time-consuming and error-prone. This system automates the process.

Key Features

  • Fleet Management - Register vehicles with dimensions and weight specs
  • Route Calculation - Automatic routing with constraint checking
  • Warning System - Alerts for bridges, tunnels, and other obstacles
  • Quote Generation - Calculate costs and export PDF quotes
  • GraphHopper Integration - Professional routing engine

Why I Built This

A family member works in the oversized transport industry and explained how much time they waste manually checking routes. I saw an opportunity to build a tool that could save hours of planning work and reduce the risk of encountering obstacles mid-transport.
