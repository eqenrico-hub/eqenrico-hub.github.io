What It Does

A chatbot system that can be loaded with custom knowledge bases to become an expert in any domain. Upload documents and the AI becomes a specialist in that content.

Problem It Solves

General AI assistants lack domain-specific expertise. This allows creating specialized assistants for specific topics, documents, or organizational knowledge.

Key Features

  • PDF and document ingestion
  • Vector-based semantic search
  • Citation of sources in responses
  • Multiple knowledge base support
  • Fine-tuned responses based on uploaded content

Tech Stack

Python LangChain ChromaDB OpenAI

Why I Built This

I needed a way to quickly create AI assistants that understand specific documentation or research materials without extensive fine-tuning.
