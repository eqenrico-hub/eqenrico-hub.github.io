What It Does

A comprehensive personal knowledge management system for organizing research notes, papers, ideas, and connections. Acts as a second brain for academic and creative work.

Problem It Solves

Research notes, papers, and ideas get scattered across tools. ATLAS provides a unified system with semantic search and AI-powered connections.

Key Features

  • Markdown-based note system
  • Bi-directional linking
  • AI-powered semantic search
  • Automatic connection suggestions
  • Research paper integration

Tech Stack

Python Obsidian Integration ChromaDB OpenAI

Why I Built This

My PhD research spans multiple topics. I needed a system that helps me see connections between ideas that I might otherwise miss.
