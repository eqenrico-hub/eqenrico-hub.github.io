What It Does

A personal AI assistant for Gmail that reads your emails, understands context, drafts smart replies, suggests calendar events, and helps you prioritize your inbox - all with human-in-the-loop approval.

Problem It Solves

Email overload is real. This app helps you focus on what matters by analyzing emails, auto-prioritizing them, drafting contextual responses, and keeping everything auditable.

Key Features

  • Gmail sync with incremental updates
  • Context-aware analysis (60-day window)
  • Smart classification (intent, urgency, required actions)
  • AI draft generation (1-3 reply variants)
  • Priority calculator and focus mode
  • Calendar intelligence for meeting requests
  • Action resolver for task extraction
  • Leaderboards/gamification
  • Full audit trail - nothing sent without approval

Tech Stack

Python FastAPI OpenAI GPT-4 Gmail API SQLite

Why I Built This

Email takes too much time. I wanted an AI assistant that understands my communication style and helps me respond faster without losing the personal touch.
