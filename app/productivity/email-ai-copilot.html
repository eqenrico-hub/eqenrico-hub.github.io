What It Does

A focused AI assistant specifically designed for composing professional emails, with tone adjustment, multiple draft options, and context understanding.

Problem It Solves

Writing professional emails takes time, especially in a second language. This provides instant, polished drafts with appropriate tone and structure.

Key Features

  • Multiple draft variations
  • Tone adjustment (formal, casual, etc.)
  • Reply context understanding
  • Multi-language support
  • Email thread summarization

Tech Stack

Python OpenAI Streamlit

Why I Built This

As a non-native English speaker, having AI assistance for professional emails helps me communicate more effectively.
